## 🌿 What's Inside

### The Tree Metaphor of Life
| Tree Stage | Life Phase | Years |
|---|---|---|
| 🌱 Seed | Conception & Infancy | 0–3 |
| 🌿 Sapling | Childhood | 3–12 |
| 🌲 Young Tree | Adolescence | 12–25 |
| 🏛️ Mature Trunk | Early Adulthood | 25–40 |
| 🌳 Full Canopy | Midlife | 40–60 |
| 🍂 Autumn Tree | Elder Years | 60–75 |
| ✨ Eternal Roots | Legacy & Beyond | 75+ |

### 9 Sections of the Guide

1. **🌱 Seed** — The Advaita foundation: *Tat Tvam Asi* (Thou Art That). Your true self is pure consciousness.
2. **🌿 Roots** — The 5 foundations of wellbeing: Body, Breath, Mind, Intellect, Bliss (Pancha Kosha).
3. **🪵 Trunk** — Core life areas: Health, Relationships, Purpose, Finance, Spirituality.
4. **🌿 Branches** — Your 75-year lifespan broken into meaningful phases with visual charts.
5. **🍃 Leaves** — Daily time allocation: how to spend 24 hours consciously.
6. **🍎 Fruit** — Ikigai: the Japanese concept of your reason for being.
7. **🌦️ Seasons** — Research tools: PERMA, Wheel of Life, Flow State, Stoicism, Mindfulness.
8. **🌲 Forest** — Advaita philosophy: Brahman, Maya, Self-Inquiry (*Neti Neti*), Non-duality.
9. **🌸 Daily Garden** — A complete daily Sadhana schedule from 5 AM to 10 PM.

## 🧘 Philosophy Foundation

This guide is grounded in **Advaita Vedanta** — the non-dual philosophy of ancient India:

- **Brahman** — The one infinite consciousness that underlies all existence
- **Atman = Brahman** — Your individual self is not separate from the universal self
- **Maya** — The illusion of separateness that causes suffering
- **Viveka** — Discriminative wisdom: discerning the real from the unreal
- **Vairagya** — Non-attachment: holding life lightly like a leaf in the wind
- **Neti Neti** — "Not this, not this" — the path of negation to find the true self
